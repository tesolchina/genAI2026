Rubric Checklist for ACE Final (Position Paper) Rubric Criteria Aspects 
1. Critical Thinking Have I: 
& Use of Grounds (40%) • Addressed all counterarguments thoroughly? • Used strong, relevant evidence (Grounds) to support claims, counterarguments and responses? • Provided effective responses to counterarguments? (Not just stating disagreement, but presenting a relevant opposing response supported with grounds and warrant)  
2. Argument Have I: 
Structure (30%) • Incorporated and logically connected all the components of Toulmin’s model (argument, claim, grounds, warrant, counterargument, and response)?  • Included a clear thesis statement in the introduction? • Added topic sentences that align with the thesis in each body paragraph? • Ended with a conclusion that summarises key points?  
3. Organisation & Is my paper: 
Clarity (20%) • Formal (no contractions, slang, or first-person pronouns)? • Clear and logically structured (no ambiguous phrasing or disjointed ideas)? • Well-organised with smooth transitions between paragraphs?  
4. Citations & Have I: 
References (10%) • Cited all sources (including secondary citation)? • Used correct APA style (signal phrases, parentheses, punctuation)? • Included an end-of-text reference list at the end of the paper?
