Analyse how the Counterargument and Response in the following example function to challenge and defend the claim. As you examine them, determine if each side employs: 
• Evidence (Grounds) to support their position, and  
• Explanation (Warrant) to logically connect the evidence to their claims. 
While chatbots may temporarily reduce feelings of loneliness by providing simulated companionship, they cannot replace the depth and reciprocity of human relationships. True friendship involves mutual understanding, emotional vulnerability, and shared experiences. Chatbots do not possess genuine emotions or consciousness. Relying on chatbots for companionship might even exacerbate loneliness in the long run by discouraging people from seeking meaningful human connections. Although chatbots are admittedly not conscious and cannot feel emotion, they can simulate depth and reciprocity by showing adaptive empathy for their users through learning user preferences and recalling personal details. All this enables chatbots to mirror human attentiveness, creating a sense of being “known”, even though they lack true comprehension. Component Example
