# Web Results: Hong Kong - Europe Relations

Date: February 11, 2026

## Summary
Collected official data portals and bureau pages that provide immigration, visitor, student visa, population, and investment information that can be filtered to European countries or used as context for HK-Europe relations. These sources can support time-series analysis, policy context, and stakeholder mapping.

## Sources

1) DATA.GOV.HK - Immigration Department datasets (provider page)
- Link: https://data.gov.hk/en-datasets/provider/hk-immd
- Summary: Open data catalog for Immigration Department, including passenger traffic and visa-related datasets.
- Excerpt: "Statistics on Daily Passenger Traffic provides... daily statistics on inbound and outbound passenger trips at all control points".
- Relevance: Primary portal for immigration and visitor flow data.

2) Statistics on Passenger, Visitor and Vehicular Traffic (dataset)
- Link: https://data.gov.hk/en-data/dataset/hk-immd-set5-statistics-passenger-visitor-vehicular-traffic
- Summary: Annual statistics on inbound/outbound passenger trips and visitor arrivals/departures.
- Excerpt: "annual statistics on (i) inbound and outbound passenger trips... (iii) arrivals and departures of visitors".
- Relevance: Can be filtered by mode and time period to analyze HK-Europe travel trends.

3) Statistics on Daily Passenger Traffic (dataset)
- Link: https://data.gov.hk/en-data/dataset/hk-immd-set5-statistics-daily-passenger-traffic
- Summary: Daily passenger traffic with breakdown by resident/visitor categories.
- Excerpt: "daily statistics on inbound and outbound passenger trips... with breakdown by Hong Kong Residents, Mainland Visitors and Other Visitors".
- Relevance: High-frequency data for short-term shocks or seasonal analysis.

4) Statistics on student visa / entry permit - applications approved (dataset)
- Link: https://data.gov.hk/en-data/dataset/hk-immd-set4-statistics-applications-approved-student
- Summary: Approved student visa / entry permit applications.
- Excerpt: "Statistics on student visa / entry permit approved provides relevant figures".
- Relevance: Supports HK-Europe education exchange analysis using regional breakdowns.

5) Census and Statistics Department - Population Estimates
- Link: https://www.censtatd.gov.hk/en/scode150.html
- Summary: Population and net movement statistics with official tables and reports.
- Excerpt: "Net movement in population" and linked tables/reports.
- Relevance: Context for migration-related trends and net movement in/out of HK.

6) Tourism Commission (policy and tourism context)
- Link: https://www.tourism.gov.hk/en/
- Summary: Tourism policy and coordination hub with links to travel and visitor information.
- Excerpt: "Tourism Commission... responsible for formulating policies and strategies... to promote the development of the tourism industry".
- Relevance: Policy context for visitor flows and tourism-related exchange.

7) Commerce and Economic Development Bureau (CEDB)
- Link: https://www.cedb.gov.hk/
- Summary: Bureau responsible for commerce, industry, and tourism policy.
- Excerpt: "Commerce and Economic Development Bureau" (official bureau portal).
- Relevance: Policy and program context for HK-Europe trade and investment relations.

8) Invest Hong Kong (InvestHK)
- Link: https://www.investhk.gov.hk/
- Summary: Investment promotion agency with annual reports and resource center.
- Excerpt: "InvestHK Achieved Outstanding Results in 2025" (resource center annual report link).
- Relevance: Investment data and reports can support HK-Europe investment flows and narratives.

## Notes for analysis
- Use Immigration Department datasets to derive EU-related travel and student flows by region (where available).
- Pair traffic data with policy timing (e.g., travel measures) from bureau releases to interpret changes.
- InvestHK reports can supply investment outcomes and sector focus relevant to EU partnerships.
