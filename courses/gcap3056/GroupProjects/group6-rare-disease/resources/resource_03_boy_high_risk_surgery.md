# Resource 3: 10-Year-Old Boy Faces High-Risk Surgery

## URL
https://www.scmp.com/news/hong-kong/health-environment/article/3149289/brave-hong-kong-boy-rare-disorder-faces-his

## Summary
The article reports on Marcus Ng, a 10-year-old boy in Hong Kong with spinal muscular atrophy (SMA), a disorder causing severe muscle weakness. With only 10% of normal lung function, he faces a high-risk spinal surgery to correct scoliosis and improve his breathing. His family acknowledges the dangers but sees the operation as necessary to stop his health from deteriorating further.

## Key Points
- **Patient**: Marcus Ng, 10 years old
- **Condition**: SMA with severe muscle weakness, only 10% normal lung function
- **Complication**: Scoliosis requiring surgical correction
- **Treatment**: High-risk spinal surgery to improve breathing
- **Family Decision**: Necessary intervention despite dangers to prevent further deterioration
