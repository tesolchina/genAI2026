# Week 5 — Plans

- **`plan_week5_AWQ_argumentation.md`** — Week 5 plan: **15 tasks** across **3 hours** (5 tasks per hour).
  - **Hour 1 — AWQ: Citations & structure:** In-text citation types, APA format, reporting verbs, full AWQ introduction, full synthesis body paragraph. Uses Sample AWQ, Student A, Articles A & B.
  - **Hour 2 — AWQ + argumentation:** Error correction (bias, paraphrasing, citations), peer review, mini AWQ (150–200 words); then “Yes, but” (concession + counterargument) and evidence-based counterargument (Module 3).
  - **Hour 3 — Argumentation + AWQ consolidation:** ACE structure (Claim, Evidence, Explanation, Rebuttal); direct quotation (AWQ-relevant); AWQ format & timing (300 words, 60 min, rubric); timed outline; recap (thesis, topic sentences, conclusion, citations, no bias).

**Goal:** AWQ tasks and practices **plus** argumentation topics (Module 3) to prepare for the **Academic Writing Quiz (in-class)** in **Week 6**.

**Materials:** Sample AWQ (Articles A & B, Student A & B), Module 1 (citations, APA, reporting verbs, quotation), Module 2 (synthesis, paraphrasing, common errors), Module 3 (rebuttal, “Yes, but,” evidence-based counterargument, ACE).
