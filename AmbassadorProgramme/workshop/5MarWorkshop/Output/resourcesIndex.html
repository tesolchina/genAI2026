Key File Locations

1. The Constraints (Read First)

  • Data/module 3/draftInstructions.md - Read this first. 100 mins, handwriting, no AI.

2. The Source Material (Read Deeply)

  • Data/Literature/HoReview.md - Your ammo. This article contains the pro/con arguments on Social Chatbots you will likely need.

3. The Technique (Study Hard)

  • Data/module 3/rubrics.md - The grading key. Note the heavy weight on "Counterargument & Response".
  • Data/module 3/argumentModels.md - The manual. Explains "Claim, Evidence, Warrant".
  • Data/module 3/strategiesCounterargument.md - Advanced tactics. How to win the argument in paragraph 3.
  • Data/module 3/AIprompts.md - Prep Tool. Use AI before the test to critique your practice work.

4. The Simulation (Do It)

  • Data/module 3/practiceWritingTask.md - Timed drill. Topic: "Working From Home". Structure: Intro + 2 Body Paras.
