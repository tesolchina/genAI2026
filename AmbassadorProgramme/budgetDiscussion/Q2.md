# Q2: This year’s Budget — where should government prioritise: compute, talent, industrial application, or regulation?

## Question

**Chinese:** 今年预算案预计继续支持AI，您认为政府最应该优先投入在算力、人才、产业应用还是监管？

**English:** This year's Budget is expected to continue supporting AI. In your opinion, which area should the government prioritize: computing power, talent, industrial application, or regulation?

---

## Simon’s initial comments

- R&D should align with the strategic goal to develop Hong Kong into an **education hub**; we do not have a strong manufacturing base, so we need to **collaborate with the Greater Bay Area**.
- Another priority is to develop **AI applications that actually create value for society**.
- **Businesses are more proactive** on this, but **government and schools (universities) are less motivated**; we need **policies and support** to change that.

## Draft answer (for interview preparation)

I wouldn’t choose a single bucket. The Budget already spreads effort across **compute** (AISC, Sandy Ridge, AI Subsidy Scheme), **talent** (Frontier Technology Scheme, HK$2b digital education, AI literacy framework), **industrial application** (AIRDI, SME digital transformation, manufacturing upgrade), and **regulation** (Ethical AI Framework, Generative AI Guideline). The more important question is **where the gaps are** and **what will unlock the rest**.

Two priorities I’d stress:

**First, industrial application and GBA collaboration.** Hong Kong’s strength isn’t mass manufacturing; it’s research, talent, capital, and our role as a **financial and education hub**. So R&D should align with that—including developing Hong Kong as an **education hub**—and we should **collaborate with the Greater Bay Area** for production, supply chains, and scale. The government is already doing some of this (e.g. HKSTP–GBA AI+ seminars, FLAIR’s industrial AI work). We need more **concrete policies and support** so that **AI applications that create real value for society** get adopted in both the public and private sectors.

**Second, who’s moving and who isn’t.** **Businesses are generally more proactive** on AI; many SMEs are already exploring or using it, though cost and access to support remain issues. **Government and universities are often less motivated**—slower to adopt and to redesign workflows. So “industrial application” isn’t only about funding; it’s about **policies and incentives** that pull the public sector and higher education into the loop. Without that, talent and compute won’t translate into enough real-world use.

On **regulation**: having a clear, proportionate framework matters for trust and for attracting investment, but Hong Kong is already building that (e.g. Ethical AI Framework, Generative AI Guideline). I wouldn’t put regulation ahead of **talent and application** in the next phase. **Talent** remains critical—both attracting and retaining AI talent and raising **AI literacy** so that decision-makers in government and schools can make good choices. So if I had to emphasise two things for the coming Budget: **industrial application (including GBA collaboration and value-creating applications)** and **policies that motivate government and universities**, alongside continued investment in talent and literacy.

## 參考答案（中文，含超連結）

我不會只選一個範疇。預算已把資源分散在**算力**（[AISC、Sandy Ridge、AI 資助計劃](https://www.info.gov.hk/gia/general/202510/15/P2025101500264.htm)）、**人才**（前沿科技支援計劃、[20 億數碼教育、AI 素養框架](https://www.info.gov.hk/gia/general/202510/15/P2025101500264.htm)）、**產業應用**（[AIRDI、中小企數碼轉型](https://www.info.gov.hk/gia/general/202510/15/P2025101500264.htm)、[製造業升級](https://www.budget.gov.hk/2025/eng/ui.html)）、以及**監管**（[倫理 AI 框架、生成式 AI 指引](https://digital.nemko.com/insights/hong-kong-ai-governance-in-2025)）。更關鍵的是**哪裡有缺口**、**什麼能帶動其餘**。

我會強調兩點：**一、產業應用與大灣區協作。** 香港的強項不是大規模製造，而是科研、人才、資本與**教育樞紐**角色；所以研發應與此對齊——包括發展香港為教育樞紐——並與[大灣區](https://www.hkstp.org/en/park-life/news-and-events/news/hkstp_gba_development_office_co-organise_greater_bay_area_ai_plus_investment_exchange_seminar)在生產、供應鏈與規模上合作。[FLAIR 等已在做工業 AI 與製造](https://www.hkpc.org/en/about-us/media-centre/press-releases/2023/flair-industrial-ai-collaborative-agreements)；我們需要更多**具體政策與支援**，讓**真正為社會創造價值的 AI 應用**在公營與私營都落地。**二、誰在動、誰未動。** [企業普遍較積極](https://hongkongbusiness.hk/economy/news/only-27-smes-hike-ai-spending-over-half-eye-adoption)，不少中小企已在探索或使用 AI，但成本與支援覆蓋仍是問題；**政府與大學往往動力不足**——採用與流程重設計都較慢。所以「產業應用」不單是撥款，還要有**政策與誘因**把公營與高教拉進圈內。**監管**方面，清晰、相稱的框架對信任與招商重要，香港已在建構；下一階段我不會把監管放在**人才與應用**之前。若要我為來年預算強調兩件事：**產業應用（含大灣區協作與創造價值的應用）** 與 **推動政府與大學的政策**，並持續投資人才與素養。
